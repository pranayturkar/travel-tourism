Ambhora(Sangam), Nagpur

Ambhora is a village situated in the Kuhi taluka of Nagpur district in the Indian state of Maharashtra. As of the 2011 Indian census, it had a population of 530

The village Ambhora Kh. is located in Kuhi Tahsil of Nagpur District in the State of Maharashtra in India. It comes under Kuhi Community Development Block. The nearest town is Bhandara, which is about 20 kilometers away from Ambhora Kh..

Social Structure of Ambhora Kh.

As per available data from the year 2009, 1417 persons live in 336 house holds in the village Ambhora Kh.. There are 688 female individuals and 729 male individuals in the village. Females constitute 48.55% and males constitute 51.45% of the total population.

Total sown/agricultural area is 540.03 ha. About 128.63 ha is un-irrigated area. About 411.4 ha is irrigated area. About 2.25 ha is irrigated by canal water. About 126.38 ha is irrigated by other sources of water.

About 5.54 ha is lying as current fallow area. About 15.44 ha is culturable waste land. About 2.25 ha is lying as fallow land other than current fallows. About 43.4 ha is covered by barren and un-cultivable land.

The village is connected by public bus services. There is a railway station more than 10 kms away from the village.
