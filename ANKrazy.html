Krazy Castle, Nagpur

From being the first rattling water slide to being the biggest play zone for children, Krazy Castle is one of the best Nagpur Amusement Parks. It has a water park that has a Fish Pool where the children can spend gala-time viewing the sea-elements like sea-horse, piranha, dolphin, mermaid, and octopus.

Individuals can laze around in water tubes inside the Lazy Crazy with their children alongside capturing their free-flowing seaside feelings enjoying crashing of the waves with varying wave-patterns in the Wave Pool. In the water-park area the latest introduction has been the Tornado slide on the topmost floor of the Splash Tower that begins the ride from a height of fifty-five feet, enters a wide bowl of forty feet, circles and exits into a pool from the colored-bowl.

Krazy Castle is one of the most widely popular Amusement Parks in Nagpur because of the family rides, a thrill zone, grooving and moving facilities it offers. You can enjoy the Sky copter here that is a ride comprising helicopter gondolas that when switched on, move in circular motions, take you at a height of forty-three feet, and rotate independently in the opposite direction. There is the Krazy Swinger incorporating the joys of a wave swinger with bum seats, resembling the ones from the movie Three-idiots.

Location: S Ambazari road, right opposite the lake of Ambazari, in Nagpur’s Ambazari, Maharashtra

Timings: The water park timings:

(April – September): 11 AM to 6 PM

(October to March): 10 AM to 5 PM

Entry Fee: For adults: (Monday to Saturday): INR 450 per person (Public holidays and Sundays): INR 490 per person

For children: INR 280 per child

For senior citizens: INR 280 per person
