Igatpuri Hillstation, Mumbai

Igatpuri Hillstation, Mumbai So, Mumbaikars, are you clinched of oh-so-common places to visit near Mumbai with family? If you scream “YES,” consider Igatpuri. It is a refreshing hill station to visit in winter as the air becomes crisp, and the monsoon also marks the arrival of lush green environs. Besides its exhilarating adventure sports, Igatpuri prevails in the fresh air in any season you visit and will most definitely leave you mesmerized.

If you love to stretch, tone, and purify your mind, body, and soul, there is a Vipassana International Academy. Attend the mindfulness classes there to harness the power of meditation. Did we mention that Igatpuri is a famous pitstop for trekkers as it boasts the highest peak of Sahyadri mountain?

Igatpuri hill station is surrounded by the highest peaks of the Western Ghats. Some places near Igatpuri are considered to be a heavenly place for the trekkers, i.e. Kalsubai, Bhandardara, Ratangad, etc. You would be glad to know that most of the Indian movie outdoor scenes especially the songs are shot here.

Igatpuri is the nearest hill station from Mumbai and famous for its scenic beauty and various types of foods like Vada pav and Idli. Igatpuri is considered as one of the best hill stations in Maharashtra to visit during the monsoons.

Best Time to Visit Igatpuri The best season to visit Igatpuri hill station is winter. The temperature is cool in the month of November, December, January, February, and March. As it is surrounded by mountains, monsoon is the best time to visit Igatpuri for trekker

Weather in Igatpuri Igatpuri is situated at an elevation of 600 meters. As it is not so high, Igatpuri experiences a mixed type of climate.

Things to Do in Igatpuri

Enjoy river rafting at the Vaitarna River

Find inner peace in Dhamma Giri – Vipassana Academy

Get absolutely drenched with the beauty of Vihigaon Falls

Watch the Camel Valley covered in dense forests and tall trees

Go for a wine tasting tour at Vallonne Vineyard

Average Temperature of Igatpuri: 24°C (approx.)

Ideal Duration: 3-4 days (approx.)

Tourists Attractions: Vaitarna River, Dhamma Giri, Vihigaon Falls

Stay Options: The Herb Farm, Rainforest, Dew Drops Boutique Resort, Mystic Valley Spa Resort

Best Time to Visit: June to November, and December-February

Igatpuri hill station is situated in the Nashik district in the state of Maharashtra. Igatpuri gathers a place among the most popular hill stations in India. Situated along the Western Ghats, Igatpuri hill station is known for the Vipassana international academy; where many people come to learn Vipassana, an ancient form of meditation.
