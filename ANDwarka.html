Dwarka River Farms, Nagpur

The Dwarka River Farms is one of the most pleasant Amusement Parks in Nagpur that offers new thrills at each and every step besides granting you hundred ways to have a refreshing getaway. It is known to be a playstation for families as it features several attractions like the Wet Bubble, Tortuga Falls and so much more being an adventure park, water, and amusement park.

You will have the opportunity to learn team-building techniques by indulging in adventure sports like bungee jumping, wall climbing, net sliding, and cycle ropeway. Spread across ten acres, this water park gives you challenges, fun, and thrills in the form of a wave pool, boating options, a universal pool meant for DJ bucket, mountain rain-dancing with a slide, and rain-dance rainbow. You and your kids can spend your entire day in this perfect venue by riding on the Dragon Train, Helicopter, and Columbus Ship. Apart from thrilling rides, there are various restaurants located on its premises that serve mouth-watering and delicious food. So if you wish to spend an entire day here then you will be delighted to know that the place offers great accommodation facilities for an overnight stay.
