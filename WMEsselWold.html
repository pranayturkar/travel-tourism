Essel World Bird Park, Borivali, Mumbai

India’s first interactive bird park is becoming of the of the best tourist attractions in Mumbai. This bird park gives you an amazing opportunity to immerse yourself in bird watching. There are a variety of birds that you can spot – Flying birds, Aquatic birds, and Terrestrial birds. The bird park has a rainforest habitat which helps the birds to develop their natural instincts.

This bird sanctuary in Mumbai comprises different terrains in accordance with the habitat requirements of the different species. These terrains range from rainforests and lakes to deserts and rocky decks, giving our visitors a feeling of being inside a Microcosmos. Apart from our varied bird species, the aviary is also home to over 150 species of special flora planted to closely mimic the natural habitats of the birds. Our collection of flora and fauna makes us the perfect tourist attraction for children in Mumbai.

Nearest Airport: 41.3 km away from Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj International Airport and can be reached in approximately 1.5 hours via taxi/private car.

Nearest Railway Station: Reach Borivali Railway Station (W) and take a taxi to reach Gorai Creek, from there take an EsselWorld Bird Park ferry which will take 10 minutes to reach.

Timings: 10:00 AM to 17:00 PM every day.

By Ferry: Reach Gorai Creek or Malad Marve to take a ferry to EsselWorld.
