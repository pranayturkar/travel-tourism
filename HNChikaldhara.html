Chikaldhara, Nagpur

Chikaldhara, standing at a height of 1118 m, wows you as soon as you find yourself here. The welcoming scent of coffee that grows in abundance here, makes you feel like the hill town is giving you a warm hug and inviting you to explore its many wonders. Some of them are enchanting views of the vastly spread hills, its many beautiful lakes and exotic species of animals and flowering plants. This is among the beautiful hill stations near Nagpur that leaves you with memories that you hold dear for long. A highly revered shrine for people of the Shaktism sect, Renuka Devi Temple is a must visit. According to historians, when Goddess Renuka was killed by her son, Parashurama; her head fell on the same place where the temple is currently located. Renuka was later given rebirth as a boon by Sage Jamadagni to his son Parashurama.

Major Attractions: Wan Sanctuary, Shakkar Lake, Gawilgarh Fort, Amner Fort, Bhimkund, Melghat Tiger Reserve

Best Time To Visit: July to September

Places To Stay: Balaji Guest House, Amruta Homestays, Hotel Vedanta

Ideal Duration: 2 to 3 days

Packages Starting From: INR 5,700

Things To Do: Visit Gavilgad, ride in a boat in Shakkar Lake, witness views from Mozart Point, stroll around Gawilgad Fort

How To Reach: The nearest railway station to reach Chikhaldara is Badnera and Akola, 80 kms away.

Distance From Nagpur: 231 km
