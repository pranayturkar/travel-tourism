WaghVilla, Nagpur

Waghville Nagpur is another option where you can go for picnic or to enjoy the various rides. The water park offers several rides that can give you a good adrenaline rush. The rides for kids and adults are different. There is also a canteen in the water park where you can go for light refreshment.

Timings: 10 am to 6 pm

Entry Fee: For adults: (Monday to Saturday): INR 450 per person (Public holidays and Sundays): INR 490 per person

For children: INR 280 per child

For senior citizens: INR 280 per person
